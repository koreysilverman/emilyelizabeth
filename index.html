EMILY ELIZABETH

Emily Elizabeth Howard is a model, digital creator, and social media personality born and raised in Florida. From a young age, she gravitated toward fashion and self-expression, building her presence on Instagram where she now commands an audience of over 3 million followers.

Known for her striking swimwear and fashion content, Emily has collaborated with major brands including Fashion Nova, Ignite Swimwear & Activewear, San Lorenzo Bikinis, Rockstar Energy, and Boutine. Her content spans editorial fashion, travel adventures, and lifestyle moments that resonate with millions worldwide.

Beyond modeling, Emily has expanded her reach across TikTok with over 415,000 followers, YouTube, and Twitter with 260K+ followers. Now based between New York City and California, she continues to push the boundaries of digital content creation while staying true to her authentic, sun-soaked aesthetic.
